## The Jedi

> There is no emotion, there is peace…  
> There is no ignorance, there is knowledge…  
> There is no passion, there is serenity…  
> There is no chaos, there is harmony…  
> There is no death, there is the Force.

The Jedi Order is an ancient monastic organization devoted to peace, knowledge, and balance in the Force. Members act as guardians, diplomats, and defenders of the Galactic Alliance.

### Skills

The Force, Meditation, Combat Style (Lightsaber), Insight, Willpower, Influence, Perception, Athletics, Endurance, Evade, Lore (Jedi Order)

---

### Membership

**Why Join:** Jedi seek mastery of mind, body, and spirit. They serve as protectors of the galaxy and strive to maintain harmony through the Force.

**Joining at Character Creation:**  
Players may select the Jedi career and begin as **Padawans** trained by a Jedi Master.

**Joining During Play:**  
Joining requires the discovery of latent Force sensitivity, sponsorship by a Jedi Knight or Master, and an oath to follow the Jedi Code.  
**Minimum Requirements:** The Force 30%, Meditation 30%, Willpower 30%.

### Restrictions and Taboos

- No personal attachments or possessions.
- Never use the Force for aggression.
- Defying the Code results in exile or loss of Force affinity.

#### Initiate – “Stretch Out With Your Feelings”

**Requirements:** The Force 30%, Meditation 30%, Willpower 30%.

**Duties:** Study the Code, meditate, perform temple tasks.

**Privileges:** Access to training for jedi skills

**Rewards:** Learn _Sense_

#### Padawan – “Your Eyes Can Deceive You”

**Requirements:** The Force 40%, Meditation 40%, Willpower 40%.

**Duties:** Accompany Knights, perform basic missions, resist emotion.

**Privileges:** Access to training for jedi skills at 10% discount plus may access materials for crafting a lightsaber for free - must provide own kyber crystal.

**Rewards:** Learn _Awareness, Telekinesis_

#### Knight – “Do or do not”

**Requirements:** 4 Jedi skills at 70%; must pass Jedi Trials.

**Duties:** Act as a peacekeeper; may take a Padawan.

**Privileges:**

- Access to training for jedi skills at 50% discount
- Free lightsaber crafting materials
- Jedi Order members will house and shelter you for free on missions

**Rewards:** Learn _Coerce, Deflect, Telepathy, Speed_ and one lightsaber form trait.

#### Master – “For My Ally Is the Force”

**Requirements:** 3 Jedi skills at 90%; must have trained a Padawan to Knighthood.

**Duties:** Lead missions, teach.

**Privileges:**

- Access to training for jedi skills at 70% discount
- Free lightsaber crafting materials
- Jedi Order and Alliance members will house and shelter you for free on missions
- Your own Jedi School

**Rewards:** Learn _Healing, Hibernation, Illusion_ and one lightsaber form trait

#### Grand Master – “Luminous Beings Are We”

**Requirements:** 2 Jedi skills at 110%; appointed by the Council.

**Duties:** Guide the Order, maintain the balance of the Force.

**Privileges:**

- Access to training for jedi skills at 100% discount
- Free lightsaber crafting materials
- Jedi Order and Alliance members will house and shelter you for free on missions, as well as providing assistance where possible
- Your own Jedi Temple

**Rewards:** Learn _Concealment, Deny, Projection_. May learn a Mastery trait for an existing form.

### Lightsaber Forms

#### Basic

##### Form 1 (Shii-Cho)

May substitute this combat style for outmaneuvering multiple opponents instead of using the Evade skill.

##### Form 2 (Makashi)

Increase weapon size by one step for the purposes of resisting the effects of the _Disarm Opponent_ special effect.

##### Form 3 (Soresu)

May use the 'accidental injury' special effect on a normal parry rather than just a critical when deflecting a blaster bolt.

##### Form 4 (Ataru)

A successful Evade roll does not force the character to end up prone.

##### Form 5 (Djem So)

At the cost of a special effect and additional action point, follow up a successful parry with an immediate counterattack. Levels of success still generate additional special effects.

##### Form 6 (Niman)

As a special effect, use a Force ability after an attack or parry

##### Form 7 (Juyo)

May use a free action point for the sole purpose of attacking if no defensive actions are taken that round. When this is used, _make an Endurance roll for Fatigue at the end of the Round_.

#### Mastery

##### Form 1 (Shii-Cho)

Attack up to 3 opponents grouped together per the “Sweep Attack” rules

##### Form 2 (Makashi)

Attacks and parries against lightsaber-wielding opponents are rolled 1 grade easier

##### Form 3 (Soresu)

Spend a special effect to have blaster bolt parries not require an action point

##### Form 4 (Ataru)

With a successful combat style roll, can instantly jump to attack another opponent up to 10m away without requiring a Move action

##### Form 5 (Djem So)

Roll your damage modifier and double the result on successful attacks

##### Form 6 (Niman)

Grants an additional action point per round

##### Form 7 (Juyo)

Use the Maximize Damage special effect without requiring a critical

## Dark Jedi

The Dark Jedi are fallen Force-users who reject the Code, wielding the Force through emotion and will. They are power-seekers, rebels, and conquerors.

---

### Skills

The Force, Meditation, Combat Style (Lightsaber), Deceit, Influence, Willpower, Endurance, Insight, Passion (Hatred or Ambition), Lore (Sith)

### Membership

**Why Join:** The pursuit of unrestrained power, independence, and mastery of forbidden Force techniques.

**Joining at Character Creation:**  
Players may select the Sith career and begin as a **Disciple** trained by a Sith.

**Joining Requirements:**  
The Force 30%, Willpower 30%, Passion (Hatred or Ambition) 40%.  
A known act of defiance against Jedi or the Republic.

#### Acolyte – “Peace Is a Lie”

**Requirements:** The Force 30%, Passion (Hatred or Ambition) 40%.

**Duties:** Serve a master, study ancient Sith lore.

**Privileges:** Training of Sith skills

**Rewards:** _Sense_

#### Disciple – “Through Passion, I Gain Strength”

**Requirements:** The Force 40%, Combat Style 40%, Willpower 40%.

**Duties:** Train under a Dark Master, embrace conflict.

**Privileges:** Training of Sith skills, free lightsaber crafting materials.

**Rewards:** _Awareness, Speed, Telekinesis_

#### Adept – “Through Victory, My Chains Are Broken”

**Requirements:** 4 skills at 70%, one personal triumph over a superior.

**Duties:** Lead lesser Acolytes, expand influence.

**Privileges:** Training of Sith skills at 25% discount, free lightsaber crafting materials.

**Rewards:** _Conceal, Deflect, Violate_ & one lightsaber form trait (see Jedi path)

#### Lord – “The Dark Side Is a Path...”

**Requirements:** 3 skills at 90%, one apprentice trained.

**Duties:** Manipulate others, expand dominion.

**Privileges:** Training of Sith skills at 50% discount, free lightsaber crafting materials, gain free board and transport when acting in service of the Sith or their Empire. Command of a minor outpost.

**Rewards:** _Aura, Deny, Illusion_ & another lightsaber form trait.

#### Master – “Unlimited Power!”

**Requirements:** 2 skills at 110%, public act of conquest.

**Duties:** Rule territories or orders; enforce loyalty through fear.

**Privileges:** Training of Sith skills at 50% discount, free lightsaber crafting materials, gain free board, transport or assistance when acting in service of the Sith or their Empire. Personal fortress.

**Rewards:** _Leech, Lightning, Transfer_ & a lightsaber mastery trait for an existing form

## The Bounty Hunter's Guild

A professional organization regulating bounty contracts across the galaxy. Members gain access to secure contracts, resources, and Guild protection.

---

### Skills

Combat Style (Ranged or Melee), Track, Stealth, Mechanics, Streetwise, Perception, Pilot (Space), Influence, Willpower, Endurance

### Membership

**Why Join:** Hunters gain profit, prestige, and support.

**Joining Requirements:**

- Track 40%, Combat Style 50%, Streetwise 40%.
- One confirmed independent bounty capture.

#### Candidate – “Target Acquired”

**Requirements:** Entry test and sponsorship.

**Duties:** Assist Hunters, study Guild code.

**Privileges:** Access to low-value bounties and Guild training.

**Rewards:** Always know the location of your current mark (planet)

#### Hunter – “No Disintegrations”

**Requirements:** Track 50%, Combat Style 50%, Streetwise 50%.

**Duties:** Take contracts, maintain discipline.

**Priviliges:** Access to mid-tier bounties. Basic protection from the Guild in case of disputes

**Rewards:** Once per session, reduce killing blow to incapacitating result.

#### Master Hunter – “I can take you in warm...”

**Requirements:** Track 70%, Combat Style 70%, Perception 70%. Successful completion of one particularly hard bounty.

**Duties:** Mentor novices, complete mid-tier targets.

**Privileges:**

- Access to the highest-value bounty postings and restricted information networks
- Use of advanced Guild resources (e.g., specialized equipment at reduced cost, advanced medical facilities)

**Rewards:** Double payment for live captures.

#### Overseer – “Or I can take you in cold”

**Requirements:** 4 relevant skills 90%, 3 major captures.

**Duties:** Lead hunts, represent the Guild.

**Privileges:**

- Access to the highest-value bounty postings and restricted information networks
- Use of advanced Guild resources (e.g., specialized equipment at reduced cost, advanced medical facilities)
- Profit share of 5% of the Guild's rewards

**Rewards:** Upgrade one combat style to 100%

#### Chapter Master – “This Is My Quarry”

**Requirements:** 4 relevant skills 110%, unparalleled reputation and success within the bounty hunting community.

**Duties:** Lead hunts, represent the Guild.

**Privileges:**

- Large share of guild profits
- Luxurious personal apartment
- Ability to command guild resources
- Access to all bounties and information the guild has to offer

**Rewards:** Raise a characteristic by 1d6 up to the species maximum.

## Smuggler's Guild

A loose confederation of pilots, runners, and traders united by freedom and profit. They straddle the line between hero and criminal.

---

### Skills

Pilot (Space), Mechanics, Deceit, Streetwise, Gambling, Commerce, Influence, Perception, Evade, Navigation, Conceal

### Membership

**Why Join:** Independence, adventure, and the thrill of fast credits.  
**Joining Requirements:** Pilot 50%, Streetwise 50%, Deceit 40%.

### Taboos

- Never betray a crewmate.
- Never cheat a fellow smuggler (unless they start it).
- Always settle your debts… eventually.

#### Rookie – “Hidden Compartments”

**Requirements:** Two skills at 40%, one successful run.

**Duties:** Run small cargoes, build contacts.

**Privileges:** Access to local training

**Rewards:** Your ship is outfitted with hidden cargo bays that can hold your party or a small amount of contraband

#### Spacer – “Old Debts, New Favors”

**Requirements:** Three skills at 50%, two successful runs

**Duties:** Manage smuggling runs, keep quiet.

**Privileges:**

- Always know a local contact (may betray you).
- Access to reduced training costs (25%)

**Rewards:** +10% to any guild skill

#### Runner – “This is where the fun begins”

**Requirements:** Two skills at 70%, three successful runs.

**Duties:** Evade authorities, maintain ships.

**Privileges:**

- The guild will help you hide from the law and sell stolen goods
- Reduced training costs (50%)
- Always know a local contact (may betray you).
- Ship repairs are 10% cheaper

**Rewards:** One pilot skill increases to 100%

#### Captain – “Never Tell me the odds”

**Requirements:** 3 skills at 90%, one daring escape

**Duties:** Lead crews, negotiate contracts.

**Privileges:**

- The guild will help you hide from the law and sell stolen goods
- Reduced training costs (50%)
- Always know a local contact (may betray you).
- Ship repairs are 20% cheaper
- One free ship modification (speed, stealth, or cargo).

**Rewards:** Lucky - When rolling for any skill test, the character can choose which way to read the d100 result. For example, a roll of 90 could be read as 09. Note: if double digits, such as 00, 11, 22, and so forth, are rolled, the result cannot be reinterpreted.

#### Legend – “Encased in Carbonite”

**Requirements:** Legendary job, 3 skills at 110%.

**Duties:** Expand Alliance influence, mentor new captains.

**Privileges:**

- The guild will help you hide from the law and sell stolen goods
- Reduced training costs (75%)
- Always know a local contact (may betray you).
- Ship repairs are 20% cheaper
- One additional free ship modification (speed, stealth, or cargo).
- If your ship is destroyed, gain a new one for free (though the previous owner may not be happy)

**Rewards:** If your character is killed, you may instead state they are merely captured. They must be rescued from where they're being stored in carbonite. This ability may only be used once.

## Mandalorian Clans

Ancient warrior families devoted to honor, combat, and “The Way.” Mandalorians are bound by creed, clan, and beskar.

---

### Skills

Combat Style (Mandalorian), Endurance, Willpower, Leadership, Mechanics, Pilot (Jetpack or Starfighter), Tactics, Athletics, Insight

### Taboos

- Never abandon a foundling.
- Never forsake “The Way.” (which differs between clans)

### Membership

**Why Join:** Honor, loyalty, and superior craftsmanship.  
**Joining Requirements:** Combat Style 50%, Endurance 50%, Willpower 50%.

#### Foundling – “This Is the Way”

**Requirements:** Clan sponsorship and acceptance.

**Duties:** Train and serve elders

**Privileges:**
Access to a beskar armorer to craft leg armor with only materials cost.

#### Warrior – “Watch Those Wrist Rockets”

**Requirements:** Combat Style 60%, Endurance 60%.

**Duties:** Fight for the clan; protect foundlings.

**Privileges:**

- Access to a beskar armorer to craft leg and arm armor with only materials cost.
- You can find a mandalorian enclave on a planet with 30% success chance

**Rewards:** 3 wrist rockets per adventure (refill each mission, as particle grenade).

#### Champion – “Weapons are part of my religion”

**Requirements:** Combat Style 70%, Willpower 60%.

**Duties:** Lead hunts, enforce honor.

**Priviliges:**

- You may carry weapons and armor even in places that are otherwise under heavy security
- Access to a beskar armorer to craft leg, arm and abdomen armor with only materials cost.
- You can find a mandalorian enclave on a planet with 30% success chance
- You can always find out where to get some beskar

**Rewards:** Increase a combat style of your choice by 10%

#### Clan Leader – "Clan of Two"

**Requirements:** 3 skills 80%, 5 years clan service.

**Duties:** Protect your clan, uphold your laws.

**Priviliges:**

- You may carry weapons and armor even in places that are otherwise under heavy security
- Access to a beskar armorer to craft any beskar armor piece with only materials cost.
- You can find a mandalorian enclave on a planet with 30% success chance
- You can always find out where to get some beskar

**Rewards:** May name one Foundling NPC who assists once per session.

#### Future Mand’alor – “It's a creed”

**Requirements:** Combat Style 110%, Leadership 110%, chosen by clans.

**Duties:** Unite the clans. Solve disputes. Pave the way.

**Priviliges:**

- You may carry weapons and armor even in places that are otherwise under heavy security
- Access to a beskar armorer to craft armor with only materials cost.
- You can find a mandalorian enclave on a planet with 30% success chance
- You can always find out where to get some beskar
- You know where the darksaber is

**Rewards:** Any missing pieces of beskar armor (including upgrading helmet to a masterwork mask with +1 armor) and can use the darksaber as part of your combat style.

## Czerka Corp

> “Ethics are expensive.” — Czerka Executive Motto

Czerka Corporation is one of the oldest and most powerful megacorporations in the galaxy, specializing in weapons, droids, mining, and colonial development. It is infamous for ruthless business tactics and moral flexibility.

### Skills

Commerce, Bureaucracy, Deceit, Influence, Knowledge (Corporate Law or Engineering), Mechanics, Computers, Negotiation, Insight

### Restrictions and Taboos

- Never act against Czerka’s financial interests.
- All discoveries, inventions, or patents belong to the Corporation.
- Disloyalty or whistleblowing is punishable by termination — literal or otherwise.

### Membership

**Why Join:** Steady credits, access to cutting-edge tech, and rapid advancement for those willing to compromise ethics.

**Joining at Character Creation:**  
Players may choose the Czerka employee background and begin as **Associates**.

**Joining During Play:**  
Requires business or technical skill and a recommendation from a Czerka representative.  
**Minimum Requirements:** Commerce 40%, Mechanics 40%, Deceit 40%.

#### Associate – “We Make It Better”

**Requirements:** Commerce 40%, Mechanics 40%, Deceit 40%.

**Duties:** Follow orders, complete assigned projects.

**Privileges:** Access to discounted Czerka equipment (10%).

**Rewards:** +10% to Commerce or Mechanics.

---

#### Manager – “Corporate Interests”

**Requirements:** Commerce 60%, Influence 50%, Bureaucracy 50%.

**Duties:** Supervise lower staff, deliver quarterly profits.

**Privileges:** Once per adventure, requisition a shipment or vehicle.

**Rewards:** Gain +10% to influence.

---

#### Director – “Buy, Sell, Dominate”

**Requirements:** 3 corporate skills 70%, at least one successful project worth over 50 000 credits.

**Duties:** Expand markets, eliminate rivals, control subsidiaries.

**Privileges:** 25% discount on Czerka gear; may command a small research team.

**Rewards:** +1 permanent Luck Point.

---

#### Executive – “Profit and Progress”

**Requirements:** 3 corporate skills 90%, one major discovery or hostile takeover.

**Duties:** Direct regional operations, enforce company doctrine.

**Privileges:** Access to Czerka’s restricted R&D catalogue; private shuttle and retinue.

**Rewards:** Gain one permanent Ally (corporate enforcer, analyst, or droid).

---

#### Board Member – “The Company Owns You”

**Requirements:** 2 key skills 110%, and unanimous Board approval.

**Duties:** Set policy, authorize acquisitions, crush rebellion.

**Privileges:** Free use of all Czerka assets within jurisdiction.

**Rewards:** May reroll one failed social or business roll per session.

## The Sith Empire

> “Strength in service. Honor in command.”

Unlike the Dark Jedi who revel in chaos, the Sith Empire is a structured, militant power that prizes discipline, hierarchy, and victory through unity. The Force is but one tool — loyalty and honor are its foundation.

---

### Skills

Oratory, Willpower, Combat Style (Imperial or Military), Knowledge (Tactics or Imperial Law), Influence, Insight, Endurance, Politics, Bureaucracy, Deceit

### Restrictions and Taboos

- Betrayal without cause or honor is forbidden.
- Cowardice is punishable by demotion or death.
- All Sith serve the Empire before themselves.

### Membership

**Why Join:** To serve a greater purpose through strength, command, and duty — and rise by merit or conquest.

**Joining at Character Creation:**  
Players may begin as **Cadets** within the Imperial military.

**Joining During Play:**  
Requires demonstration of loyalty and skill to an Imperial commander.  
**Minimum Requirements:** Willpower 50%, Combat 50%, Leadership 40%.

#### Cadet – “For the Empire”

**Requirements:** Willpower 40%, Combat Style 40%, Discipline 40%.

**Duties:** Train, obey, and uphold Imperial law.

**Privileges:** Imperial stipend, training access.

---

#### Soldier – “The Empire Endures”

**Requirements:** Combat Style 50%, Willpower 50%, Endurance 50%.

**Duties:** Serve on campaigns, maintain discipline.

**Privileges:** Standard-issue armor and weaponry; medical support.

**Rewards:** +10% Willpower.

---

#### Knight-Commander – “Through Strength, Victory”

**Requirements:** 3 skills 70%, one notable victory or service medal.

**Duties:** Command troops, enforce honor.

**Privileges:** Leadership training; access to Imperial troops.

---

#### Lord-Marshal – “Through Duty, Power”

**Requirements:** 3 skills 90%, one successful planetary campaign.

**Duties:** Oversee sectors, train successors, advise the Emperor.

**Privileges:** Personal ship and command garrison.

**Rewards:** The character can endure any pain. He never needs to check Endurance to prevent unconsciousness when wounded, and is immune to torture.

---

#### Emperor’s Hand – “Honor Is Strength”

**Requirements:** 2 skills 110%, chosen by the Emperor.

**Duties:** Carry out high-level missions of state.

**Privileges:** Temporary command of fleets, legions, and governors.

**Rewards:** The character is immediately aware of the enemies of the Sith Empire and those who would directly harm him.

## The Galactic Alliance

> “We are the spark that will light the fire to restore the Republic.”

The Galactic Alliance represents the ideals of liberty and cooperation among free worlds. Its members — soldiers, diplomats, and Jedi alike — strive to protect the galaxy from tyranny and chaos.

---

### Skills

Oratory, Science(Tactics), Customs (Galactic Law), Politics, Pilot (Starfighter or Transport), Influence, Insight, Willpower, Persuade, Endurance, Bureaucracy, Commerce

### Restrictions and Taboos

- Abuse of authority is treason.
- Aid all civilians in distress when possible.
- Loyalty to democracy above personal gain.

### Membership

**Why Join:** To defend freedom, serve the greater good, or redeem the galaxy’s mistakes.

**Joining Requirements:** Leadership 40%, Oratory 50%, Customs (Galactic Law) 50%.

### Skills

Science (Tactics), Willpower, Oratory

#### Recruit – “We Are the Spark”

**Requirements:** Leadership 40%, Willpower 40%.

**Duties:** Serve loyally in any capacity.

**Privileges:** Access to Alliance training and rations.

#### Officer – “A New Hope”

**Requirements:** Two cult skills at 50%

**Duties:** Lead squads, maintain morale.

**Privileges:** Command small units temporarily; requisition basic equipment.

**Rewards:** +10% Willpower

#### Commander – “May the Force Be With You”

**Requirements:** Three skills at 70% one successful major mission.

**Duties:** Coordinate operations and logistics.

**Privileges:** May bring an NPC soldier on an adventure if available; requisition equipment.

**Rewards:** +1 permanent Luck Point.

#### General – “Hold On, We’re Coming”

**Requirements:** Three skills at 90%, 3 years command experience.

**Duties:** Command fleets or planetary forces.

**Privileges:** Call in reinforcements or air support once per campaign arc.

**Rewards:** Gain a NPC soldier who accompanies you on every adventure

#### Chief of State – “For the Good of the Galaxy”

**Requirements:** Two skills at 110%, elected by Council vote.

**Duties:** Represent the Alliance and maintain unity.

**Privileges:** Diplomatic immunity, access to Alliance treasury and fleets.

**Rewards:** The character can accomplish incredible feats or inspire others to through sheer force of will. A successful Willpower roll is needed for the desired effect. They might lead an army across frozen tundra in the dead of winter without any deserters, or overcome a failed Athletics roll to climb a summit. GM's discretion on the difficulty of the task. Only usable once per game session. Cost: one or more levels of Fatigue as decided by the GM.
